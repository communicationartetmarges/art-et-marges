L’ÉTRANGE COLLECTION DE MAMITA

VISIT AND WORKSHOP FOR CHILDREN

SATURDAY SEPTEMBER 9, 2PM-4PM

What does it mean to collect? What can be collected? And who collects what? Do you collect? What does that say about you? What does it say about you? And the museum, the museum has a collection, a collection of what?

To mark the release of their book "L'étrange collection de Mamita", authors Lisbeth Renardy and Thomas Medard have put together a drawing workshop inspired by their book. The workshop is preceded by a visit to the exhibition, punctuated by excerpts from the story, featuring a gallery of characters with truculent collections.

in FR | Activity for children aged 5 to 10 | 3€ per child | book here | Signing session after the workshop. "L'étrange collection de Mamita" is published by Les Fourmis Rouges.

MEET SHEN ÖZDEMIR

BRUSSELS GALLERY WEEKEND OFF PROGRAM

SUNDAY SEPTEMBER 10, 2PM

Shen Özdemir, a recent graduate of Arts au Carré, is already at the head of more than twenty troupes of KARNAVALO, the syncretic folklore she has imagined so that everyone can find a place in it. Last year she was part of the Génération Brussels exhibition, this year she's featured in the BRUSSELS GALLERY WEEKEND off program, at the ART ET MARGES MUSEUM.

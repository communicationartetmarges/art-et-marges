JEAN-MARIE MASSOU

WEDNESDAY, 23 NOV 2022 3PM-8:30PM (WITH AFTER PARTY)

WOENSDAG 23.11.2022 15U-20U30

5:30 pm Book signing of JEAN-MARIE MASSOU (Collectif La Belle Brute) and LA DEVINIÈRE (Vincen Beeckman)

6:30 pm and 8 pm André Bargues and the collective La Belle Brute play Massou! André Bargues, former mayor of Marminiac and accordionist, and the collective La Belle Brute have founded since Jean-Marie Massou's death in 2020 the association of Massou's friends: Le Sidéral for the diffusion and preservation of his work. They will play together some of Jean-Marie's favorite tunes in his honor.

Before and between the concerts The DJset of Philippe de la Devinière

9pm After party at the PETIT LION with the famous duo of disc jockeys PIPO and BIMBO (rue Haute 232, 1000 Brussels)

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit.

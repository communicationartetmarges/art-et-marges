SCREENING AT CINÉMA NOVA: LA DEVINIÈRE BY BENOÎT DERVAUX

THURSDAY JAN 12, 2023 7PM

"On February 18, 1976, La Devinière, a place of institutional psychotherapy, opened its doors to 19 children deemed incurable and rejected by all. Neither common sense, nor psychiatry, nor pedagogy could admit or recognize them. These children, in short exiled, La Devinière accepted them definitively with the founding principle of not rejecting them under any pretext. The word "asylum" takes on its true meaning, a space without grids or chemicals, where one is given the right to "live with one's madness". For more than twenty years, bonds of solidarity have been forged between those who had nothing to link them. As the seasons passed, I filmed this place as closely as possible, which brought back life where everything seemed doomed." (Benoît Dervaux) The screening is scheduled on the occasion of the exhibition SI TU N'VIENS PAS J'TE SCALPE at the ART ET MARGES MUSEUM. An exhibition that presents portraits and installations of residents of the DEVINIÈRE, orchestrated by the Brussels photographer VINCEN BEECKMAN.

2000, BE, VO FR, 90'.
Screening in the presence of Dominique Daue (director of La Devinière) and Benoît Dervaux (filmdirector) - At Cinema Nova, rue d'Arenberg 3, 1000 Brussels.

PHOTO | BRUT BXL STUDY DAYS AT CIVA

FRIDAY 27 AND SATURDAY OF JANUARY 28, 2023 9AM-6:30PM

PHOTO | BRUT BXL is a project of exhibitions and multidisciplinary events coordinated by the Centre d'Art Brut et Contemporain La « S » Grand Atelier in collaboration with Bruno Decharme, collector and founder of abcd-art brut in Paris, and 4 partner organisations based in Brussels: CENTRALE for contemporary art, Botanique , Art et marges museum and Tiny Gallery.
The exhibitions run from Nov 24, 2022 to Mar 19, 2023.

Bruno Decharme, Anne-Françoise Rouche and their partners invite you to participate in two days of sharing and reflection around the concept of art brut.

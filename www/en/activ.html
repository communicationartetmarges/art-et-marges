BLINDFOLDED VISIT

12.12.2021 2-4PM

WITHIN THE FOLDS OF TIME is the exhibition that confuses your senses! In this unusual guided tour, you will experience the works through touch, hearing, taste and smell... blindfolded. More than ever, you'll let yourself slip WITHIN THE FOLDS OF TIME...

The visit was conceived thanks to the expertise of Suzanne Giovannini, architect and lecturer at the ULB, specialized among other things in the apprehension of space by people with a visual handicap.

In French - All publics - 7€/pers. - on reservation at accueil@artetmarges.be.

MIDDAY WITHIN THE FOLDS OF TIME

12.21.2021, 01.18 and 02.15.2022 12:30 PM-01:30 PM

Get away from the daily hustle and bustle.

Every third Tuesday of the month, the Art et marges museum organizes a timeless moment combining movement and breath. The session, inspired by yoga and qi gong techniques, is led by Mario Zucconi, whose approach is also nourished by contemporary dance, eutony, functional movement and classical dance.

The practice is open to all, 10€ per session, on reservation at accueil@artetmarges.be.

GONG BATH

03.08.2022 7-8PM

For one evening, the exhibition WITHIN THE FOLDS OF TIME will be the setting for a unique experience... The Gong Bath is a collective treatment where all the people lie on yoga mats and let the sound and vibrations of the gongs flow through them.

The sound of the gong allows a cleansing of emotional memories and a harmonization of the energetic bodies with the physical body, which allows the release of blockages. Thus, it is an excellent therapy for all problems related to stress, depression, fatigue, feelings of separation, loneliness, anger, anxiety or simply to relax.

The session will be guided by Nirmal Karam, Kundalini yoga teacher and gong master. 30€/pers., reservation at accueil@artetmarges.be.

HERBAL TEA IN A FOLD OF TIME

EVERY THURSDAY 3PM

Every week, we activate our herbal tea fountain. The recipes were collected by Clémence Estingoy, Maxence Doucet and Juliette Brishoual, the trio of artists who transformed our lab space into a greenhouse where you can take your time, feel good, and leave a recipe for herbal tea...

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.

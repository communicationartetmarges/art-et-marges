NOCTURNES

TUESDAY MARCH 27, 6:00-10:00 PM

The Art et marges museum is taking part in the Brussels museums Nocturnes program. For the occasion, the museum will be plunged into darkness! Enter the colourful swarms through your smartphone’s light beam. In the chiaroscuro, Michel Goyon reveals his drawings: interlocking shapes, visual games and wordplay, plastic findings, numbers and phosphorescent surprises are inspired by art brut, American comics and scientific literature.

Two guided tours by Michel Goyon at 6pm and 8pm (in FR - limited capacity - register on site during the Nocturne - be sure to arrive well in advance).

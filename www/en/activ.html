Vernissage « L’Amérique n'existe pas ! »

Thursday 3 October 2019 6 PM -9 PM

Launch of the book: "DES PÉPITES DANS LE GOUDRON ! Un roadtrip brut en Amérique" (Knock Outsider).

19:30 Concert "Il était dans l’Ouest une fois"

It’s a musical alliance of three men, slide guitars and homemade electric horses which will draw far slower than their shadow their improvised soundtrack with rabbit sauce…

With:
Kostia Botkine (singer - Choolers Division - BE),
Julien Bancilhon (homemade guitars - Is a Fish / One Lick Less - FR),
Wilfrid Morin (homemade electric horse - Keeper Volant - FR/BE)

A tape produced by La Belle Brute will be available on the vernissage.

Special thanks to Antoine Boulangéand la "S" Grand Atelier.

After party in café «Le Petit Lion» (232 rue Haute) with dj Blondie Warholles

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.

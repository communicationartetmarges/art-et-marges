DIMANCHE PLUS QUE GRATUIT | VIDÉOMUZ FOR KIDS

SUNDAY, DECEMBER 3 11AM-6PM

Every first Sunday of the month, admission to the museum is free. And on this first Sunday in December, we'd like to take the opportunity to introduce you to the VidéoMuz project, designed in conjunction with the Art et marges museum collection.
Since September, a team of young people from MJ88 (in our neighborhood) have been hard at work creating a video game that explores the notion of Art brut. Throughout the day, come and visit the exhibition DON'T CALL IT ART BRUT free of charge and experience this unclassifiable raw video game for yourself!

As part of the participatory digital program VideoMuz For Kids , with Art&Publics, Action Média Jeunes and Maison de jeunes 88

Guided tour at 2 p.m. (in het FR) : reservation at accueil@artetmarges.be
Free for all

BLURRY LANDSCAPES | LA JUNGLE
VERNISSAGE AND ALBUM PRESENTATION

TUESDAY, DECEMBER 12, 2023 6-9PM

The Art et marges museum, We Insist Boutique! and the duo La Jungle invite you to the opening of their exhibition BLURRY LANDSCAPES. Jim and Roxie, from La Jungle, will present their album in the form of a short talk. They'll talk about their long collaboration with over twenty Art brut artists.

The disc presentation will take place at the museum at 6pm. The exhibition of original works around Blurry Landscapes will be on view at We Insist! (our neighbors: rue Haute, 306) at the same time. Note to visitors: La Jungle will not be playing live that evening, but you can take home a copy of the record in cd or vinyl format.

Free of charge | No reservation required

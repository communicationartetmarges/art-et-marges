SOLD OUT! FANZINE WORKSHOP

SUNDAY, MARCH 10 2:00-3:30 PM

The Art et marges museum is currently presenting an inspiring series of comic strips created by Norbert Moutier between the ages of 5 and 19, with the help of his mother Simonne. During the Fanzines workshop, we'll draw our inspiration from American comics, just as he did, to create fanzines on our own, with friends or family.

GUIDED TOUR | NORBERT MOUTIER'S COMICS

SUNDAY APRIL 7, 3:00-4:00 PM

In 2020, Xavier Girard, an art teacher in Orléans, made a lucky discovery at a flea market: a thousand carefully archived homemade comic strips. They turn out to be the work of Norbert Moutier, bookseller and Z-series filmmaker, who produced them from the age of 5 to 19, assisted by his mother.

Xavier Girard invites you to join him on a guided tour of Moutier's work and his astonishing discovery.

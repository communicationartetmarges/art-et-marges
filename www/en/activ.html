MUSEUM NIGHT FEVER | KARNAVALO DRAG SHOW

SATURDAY OCTOBER 21, 7 PM-1 AM

What happens when the drag scene meets artist Shen Özdemir's imaginary carnival? The Karnavalo Drag Show! Performers from the Crash Test collective take on the colorful world of the Belgian-Turkish artist, donning coats specially created for the occasion by fashion design students from the Haute École Fransisco Ferrer. Get ready for a quirky show that reflects the vivacity of Brussels' creative scene, as evidenced by the synergy between the Karnavalo Drag Show partners!

SOLD OUT! DIA DE MUERTOS | MAGIC BANNERS WORKSHOP

SATURDAY NOVEMBER 4, 3-6PM

Make your wish to the spirits! What are the things you wish for yourself and the world? Using artist Shen Özdemir's colorful flags as inspiration, we'll create assemblies with magical properties to take with us in the DIA DE MUERTOS procession organized in the Marolles district (where the ART ET MARGES MUSEUM is located).

Solo, with friends or family, during this three-hour workshop, you can take as much time as you need to make the banner, with no obligation to stay for the duration of the workshop.

5€/pers. (3€ with the Pass Cultuur Marolles) | on reservation here | age 5+, children accompanied by an adult also taking part in the workshop.

RELEASE OF L'HAHA MAGAZINE # 1 | LE RANCH DE JEAN SMILOWSKI

BEFORE THE OPENING

THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 9, 5:30 PM

Haha is a paper magazine that publishes a yearly study of a highly atypical place in context. For several years now, it has pursued an intermittent and itinerant work of inventory, fieldwork and archiving, aimed at identifying and documenting various forms of geographical and architectural apax.

The first issue follows in the footsteps of a shack destroyed some forty years ago, which was once the home of Jean Smilowski, the Robinson of old Lille as he was called by his "discoverers"...

In collaboration with ISELP | in French | Free with reservation at accueil@artetmarges.be.

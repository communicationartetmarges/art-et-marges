SATURDAY NOVEMBER 2, 2024 3:00-6:00 PM

The Day of the Dead commemorates the passage of the dead to another world. What might it look like? What's in it?

Draw on the extraordinary imagination of artist Michel Goyon to imagine this other world, whether fairy-tale or futuristic. Using the paper-cut technique developed by the artist, create an enchanted lantern to take with you to the procession.

OPENING DON’T CALL IT ART BRUT | HIGHLIGHTS #4

THURSDAY FEBRUARY 8, 2024 6:00-8:30 PM

DON'T CALL IT ART BRUT is an evolving exhibition that gives pride of place to the het Art et marges museum collection, while reserving several spaces for discoveries and cartes blanches. Don't miss the fourth and final Highlights of the exhibition:

THE COMIC STRIPS OF NORBERT MOUTIER

In 2020, Xavier Girard, an art teacher in Orléans, made a happy discovery at a flea market: thousand carefully sorted homemade comic strips that turned out to be the diligent work of a child (from age 5 to 19) assisted by his mother.

MY FRIENDS' FRIENDS | CARTE BLANCHE TO ÉRIC CROES

The Brussels-based artist presents his Garbage series among a selection of ceramic works gleaned from the collection of the het Art et marges museum as well as from artists and workshops associated with it.

MARION OSTER | LABO SPACE

For the 4th Highlights of the DON'T CALL IT ART BRUT exhibition, the LABO space is transformed into a showcase for a set of flamboyant ex-votos by artist Marion Oster.

Free opening | no reservation needed

DIA DE MUERTOS

WORKSHOP: SOUVENIRS TO GO

SATURDAY NOVEMBER 05, 2022 BETWEEN 3:00 PM and 6:00 PM

On November 5th, Mexico invites itself to the MAROLLES. The DIA DE MUERTOS procession will be held to honor our deceased with music and colors. A celebration to remember and share. For the occasion, we propose to make mini altars to take with you, in the form of small suitcases decorated with your most moving memories.

All audiences from 5 years old (children must be accompanied). Book here and come whenever you want between 3pm and 6pm. All the program of the DIA DE MUERTOS: https://www.diademuertos.be/ (the procession will start at 6:30 pm from the park of the Hal Gate)

FILM PROJECTION AND GUIDED TOUR

SUNDAY OCT 2, 2022 3:00 PM AND SUNDAY NOV 13, 2022 2:00 PM

The artists Clara Beaudoux and Adina Ionescu-Muscel have been selected by GSARA to create an experimental film based on the work of ARIANE BERGRICHTER. The screening of their film will be followed by a guided tour of the exhibition SOURIEZ J'ADORE! by Manuela Servais, the daughter of the artist Ariane Bergrichter.

FR - registration required: accueil@artetmarges.be

Oct 2, 2022: Free (first Sunday of the month)

Nov 13, 2022: 4€ (full price)/2€ (reduced price)

GROUPS AND GUIDED VISITS

VISUALLY IMPAIRED PUBLIC

(only in FR) Our FR guides are trained in audio description, do not hesitate to contact us for a customized visit: alix.hubermont@artetmarges.be

Adults and Higher Education

The approach of our guided tours customized for the group and open to a vulnerable public as well is to delve into the work first and then explore the artist and his or her creative context.

Children and teenagers

School and extra-curricular visits, as of 4 years of age, customized for the participants (age, level and type of education, etc.) and the context of the visit. Renewed educational material for each visit stimulates the eye and sensitivities of younger children (kindergarten, primary school), whilst interaction takes pride of place for visits by teenagers.

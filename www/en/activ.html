POP-UP AND BOOKBINDING WORKSHOP CYCLE @ STERPUT

SUNDAY, FEBRUARY 9 2:00-5:00 PM
AND SUNDAY, MARCH 9 2:00-4:00 PM

How do you create and bind an image in volume that unfolds with movement? What kind of simple techniques are there to create a pop-up?
Artists Naïla Akli and Zoë Quesnel will teach you how to create pop-ups in the style of Michel Goyon, and how to bind them together in a two-part workshop.

The workshops take place at Sterput (Place du Jeu de Balle, 68 - 1000 Brussels), 350 m from the het Art et marges museum. To immerse yourself in the MICHEL GOYON exhibition beforehand, meet on 09.02 at 1.30pm at the Art et marges museum (rue Haute, 314 - 1000 Brussels).

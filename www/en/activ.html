LISTENING SESSION IN THE DARK OF JEAN-MARIE MASSOU'S TAPES

FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 17, 2023 6:00-7:00 PM

He called them ‘the rolls’ These recordings are as varied as they are precise: they are recordings of songs and laments, messages to humanity, sketches, sound novels and recordings of his daily meetings and reflections, just like a diary. He had developed a singular recording practice with several tape recorders, reinventing a mechanical sampling. He would sometimes record inside or above a cistern in order to find a particular resonance.
Matthieu Morin and Olivier Brisson, from La Belle Brute, will make you listen to some selected pieces of this singular work.

On registration : accueil@artetmarges.be - 7€/pers.

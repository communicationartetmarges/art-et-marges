INFO COVID-19

As the capacity is less than 50 people, the Covid Safe Ticket is not required to visit the ART ET MARGES MUSEUM. You just need to wear your mask to walk through our exhibition spaces. Visitors with reservation are prioritary (reservation is mandatory for groups of at least 10 people*), card payments are preferred.

*ATTENTION: if you want to organize a guided tour, please contact directly: alix.hubermont@artetmarges.be (all information under the tab ACTIVITIES)

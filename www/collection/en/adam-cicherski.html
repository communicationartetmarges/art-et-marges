Adam CICHERSKI

(Poland, 1965-)

Adam Cicherski paints small, colourful pictures in his Polish studio. His favourite subjects are modes of transport (from trams to flying saucers), sometimes featuring their brand logos. Writing plays an important role in his work. It allows him to add humorous commentary or even cover an entire surface, with typography playing a visual role. Adam Cicherski has also created a literary work, which he dreams of publishing. In it, he denounces the excesses of industrialised society.

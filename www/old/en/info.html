Support us

You appreciate the philosophy of the Art et marges museum, you enjoy its exhibitions and wish to be further surprised by its activities? Then at heart you truly are a friend of the museum!

The only thing left for you to do is to join the Friends of the Art et marges museum Association.

As a member, you will not only support the museum’s activities but also enjoy VIP status, which includes benefits and exclusive activities.

Your advantages :

  • Free entrance to the Art et marges museum
  • Invitations to the exhibition previews
  • Free entrance to the MADmusée (Liege) and the Dr. Guislain Museum (Ghent)
  • 20% discount on catalogues edited by the Museum
  • Free access to Museum activities (symposiums, Sunday aperitif-meetings, playful tours…)
  • Discount on organized group visits and anniversaries

Friends' activities

  • Guided visits by the artist and/or the curator for each exhibition
  • Cultural trips in and outside Belgium
  • Art et marges museum’s extramural exhibitions
  • Workshop visits
  • Meetings and exchanges with artists and actors of the world of art and outsider art (psychiatrists, writers…)

Annual subscription :

  • Individual 30 €
  • Family* 50 € (2 adults and children)
  • -25 years old 15 €
  • Company >250 €
  • Donating member : from 60€
